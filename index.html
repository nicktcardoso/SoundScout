About SoundScout

SoundScout is a personalized music discovery app designed to connect Spotify users with the latest releases from their favorite artists. By integrating with the Spotify Web API, SoundScout allows users to log in, view a curated dashboard of their preferred artists, and explore newly released tracks tailored to their musical tastes. Whether you're looking to stay updated on your favorite genres or discover fresh sounds, SoundScout makes it effortless to keep your playlist exciting.
